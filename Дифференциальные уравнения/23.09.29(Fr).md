1. $xydx=(x^{2}-y^{2})dy$
   $xy=(x^{2}-y^{2})\frac{dy}{dx}\;|:x\neq0$
   $\frac{y}{x}=(1-(\frac{y}{x})^{2})\frac{dy}{dx}$
   $t=\frac{y}{x}\quad y=tx\quad dy=xdt+tdx$
   $\frac{dy}{dx}=x\frac{dt}{dx}+t$
   $t=(1-t^{2})(xt'+t)$
   $t=xt'-t^{2}xt'+t-t^{3}$
   $xt'(t^{2}-1)=t$
   $\frac{dt}{dx}=\frac{t}{x(t^{2}-1)}$
   $\frac{(t^{2}-1)dt}{t}=\frac{dx}{x}$
   $tdt-\frac{dt}{t}=\frac{dx}{x}$
   $\frac{1}{2}t^{2}-\ln{|t|}=\ln{|x|}$
   $\frac{e^{\frac{y^{2}}{2x^{2}}}}{y}=1$
   
1. $(x-1)y'+3x+2y+3=0$
   $y'=\frac{3x+2y+3}{1-x}$
   $\begin{cases}3x+2y+3=0\\1-x=0\end{cases}$
   $\begin{cases}x=1\\ y=-3\end{cases}$
   $\begin{cases}u=x-1\\ v=y+3\end{cases}$
   $y=v-3\quad y'=v'$
   $v'=\frac{3(u+1)+2(v-3)+3}{1-(u+1)}$
   $v'=\frac{3u+2v}{u}$
   $\frac{dv}{du}=\frac{3u+2v}{u}$
   $\frac{dv}{3u+2v}=\frac{du}{u}$
   $u=zv\quad du=zdv+vdz$
   $\frac{dv}{3zv+2v}=\frac{zdv+vdz}{zv}$
